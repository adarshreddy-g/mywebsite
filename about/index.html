About me

Hi there

I am Adarsh, A passionate guy who loves to code and tackle some real life challenges. Right from my childhood I was very much interested in computers and very enthusiastic to learn about this so called device on which the entire world is running. I still remember one of my good olden days where I had tried my level best in dismantling one such computer just to know its inner story. Though this action of mine spoiled a computer but this amusing thing made me learn about the hardware of a computer. Gradually I became well aware of hardware and now along side dismantling I can also repair my computer. But this didn’t give me satisfaction and made me explore more and more on this digital world. I came to know about coding and how such lines of code are making miracles and reducing human efforts in various ways. My research about coding build my passion towards Computer Science.

I am a fresher, eagerly waiting to work in a challenging environment that can improve my skills and provide me a scope to learn many new technologies.
